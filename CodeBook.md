# Codebook


## Identification fields:
Columns that describe de observation

1. subject - participant ID
2. ActivityID - Numeric ID of the activity while the measurement
3. ActivityName - The activity label (text) while during the measurement

	One of:
	1. WALKING
	2. WALKING_UPSTAIRS
	3. WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
	4. SITTING
	5. STANDING
	6. LAYING
